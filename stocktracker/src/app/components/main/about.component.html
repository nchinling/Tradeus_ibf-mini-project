What is Tradeus?


Tradeus is a Singapore-based financial portfolio technology company that empowers people to take control of their financial future. Tradeus enables users to track market data (currently US data), construct, and monitor their personal portfolio. The total and annualised returns are automatically generated using current market data. Going forward, Tradeus will provide proprietary research models which enable users to build their own institutional-quality portfolio. Tradeus is 100% free-to-use, providing users with unfettered access to quality financial data and portfolio management.
